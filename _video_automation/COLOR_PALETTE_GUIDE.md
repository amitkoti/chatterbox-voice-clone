## üé® OPTION 1: WARM MODERN ‚≠ê **RECOMMENDED**
**Perfect for: Educational platforms, modern startups, growth-focused content**

### Colors
```
Background:    #FEFAF2  Soft warm cream (very inviting)
Secondary BG:  #F9F5EB  Warmer cream shade
Cards:         #FFFFFF  Pure white

PRIMARY (Deep Teal):
  Main:        #1F6F6C  Professional teal (headings, borders)
  Dark:        #12514F  Deeper teal (accents)
  Light:       #B8E7E5  Pale teal (highlights)

ACCENT (Coral):
  Main:        #FF6B6B  Vibrant coral (buttons, CTAs)
  Dark:        #EE5252  Deep coral
  Light:       #FFE0E0  Pale coral

TEXT:
  Primary:     #2D3748  Charcoal (main text)
  Secondary:   #718296  Warm grey (supporting text)
  Accent:      #1F6F6C  Teal (links, highlights)

EXTRAS:
  Gold:        #FFB74D  Golden yellow
  Purple:      #9C88FF  Soft purple
```

**Why this works:**
‚úÖ Cream + Teal = Modern, fresh, professional
‚úÖ Coral accent = Energetic, friendly, approachable
‚úÖ Perfect balance: Not too corporate, not too playful
‚úÖ Great readability with charcoal text on cream

## üéØ MY RECOMMENDATION

Based on modern educational platform design and your request for cream + complementary colors:

### **GO WITH: WARM MODERN** (`--brand warm`)

**Why?**
1. ‚úÖ **Cream background** (#FEFAF2) - Warm, inviting, not stark white
2. ‚úÖ **Deep teal** (#1F6F6C) - Modern, professional, friendly
3. ‚úÖ **Coral accent** (#FF6B6B) - Energetic, approachable, warm
4. ‚úÖ **Perfect balance** - Professional enough for B2B, friendly enough for education
5. ‚úÖ **Excellent readability** - Charcoal text on cream = perfect contrast
6. ‚úÖ **Modern aesthetic** - Similar to Notion, Airtable, modern startups

**This palette:**
- Works beautifully for Data Engineering content
- Feels warm and inviting (cream + coral)
- Looks professional and modern (teal)
- Has great energy without being overwhelming
- Perfect for growth-focused presentations
